Trailblaze

Trailblaze 2.0 — our exclusive multi-disciplinary competition featuring Debate, Quiz, Dance, Film Making, Business Simulation, and Science Exhibition.

Building on the success of its debut edition, Trailblaze 2.0 offers an even greater opportunity for students to collaborate, compete, and showcase their talents across diverse fields. Get ready to shine brighter than ever!

Participate in exciting competitions across various domains and showcase your talents. Win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your skills.

The DEBATE

REASONED REVOLUTION

Dialectica follows the Asian Parliamentary Debate format, encouraging structured, persuasive, and logical argumentation. Students explore diverse perspectives on pressing global and social issues. The event builds skills in critical thinking, public speaking, and teamwork. Debaters learn to stay composed under time constraints while respecting opposing viewpoints. Dialectica aims to develop future leaders who communicate with clarity, empathy, and conviction.

THE QUIZ

MIND MAZE

Mind-Maze is a high-energy quiz that tests awareness, intellect, and quick thinking across multiple subjects. Participants tackle questions from current affairs, pop culture, science, logic, and general knowledge. With written, audio-visual, and buzzer rounds, the event promises an exciting and varied challenge. Teams must collaborate effectively and think on their feet under time pressure. Mind-Maze creates a spirited environment that celebrates curiosity, strategy, and sharp recall.

THE POETRY RECITATION

VOICES IN VERSE

Voices in Verse celebrates spoken word artistry and emotional expression through poetry. Participants convey stories, reflections, and feelings using tone, rhythm, and performance. The event encourages creativity and helps students discover their unique poetic voice. Stage presence and interpretation play key roles in connecting with the audience. Voices in Verse highlights the power of language to inspire, move, and transform.

THE SHORT FILM FESTIVAL

CELLULOID: FILMLETS

Celluloid: Filmlets challenges young creators to interpret the theme “One Second Before.” Teams craft films that capture suspense, anticipation, or critical moments before a turning point. Participants must balance creative storytelling with technical filmmaking skills. The event promotes teamwork as students script, shoot, and edit their own cinematic pieces. Filmlets nurtures innovation and visual expression through impactful short films.

THE REEL MAKING

CELLULOID: REELRUSH

ReelScape is a fast-paced on-the-spot reel-making challenge held during Daksh and Open Day. Participants receive the theme on the morning of the event, ensuring spontaneous creativity. They must conceptualize, film, and edit a 30–90 second reel within a strict three-hour window. The competition captures campus energy, vibrance, and real-time storytelling. ReelScape rewards creativity, adaptability, and strong visual communication.

THE DANCE EVENT

NRITYA KSHETRA

Nritya Kshetra celebrates Indian dance through expressive choreography set to Indian film songs. Teams must creatively integrate a mandatory prop into their performance concept. The event values teamwork, synchronization, and innovative stage presentation. Participants combine traditional and modern elements to captivate the audience. Nritya Kshetra honors the discipline, cultural richness, and artistic power of Indian dance.

THE BUSINESS SIMULATION

CORPORATE INFERNO

Corporate Inferno puts students in the shoes of CEOs navigating real-world business challenges. Participants tackle case studies, crisis scenarios, and strategic decision-making tasks. Teams pitch their solutions before investor-style panels, sharpening communication skills. The event demands analytical thinking, adaptability, and confident presentation. Corporate Inferno blends leadership, negotiation, and creativity in a realistic corporate setting.

THE VIRTUAL FOOTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP (FIFA 2025)

E-SPORTS

E-Sports brings the excitement of football into a competitive digital environment. Players battle in 1v1 or 2v2 FIFA 25 knockout rounds, where strategy and precision are crucial. Quick reflexes and smart gameplay determine who advances through the brackets. The event is ideal for both seasoned gamers and casual competitors. VirtuLeague emphasizes sportsmanship, tactical thinking, and intense, immersive matches.

THE SCIENCE FAIR

INNOVATE

Innovate encourages students to apply scientific principles to create meaningful real-world solutions. Participants present original working models that demonstrate innovation and analytical thinking. The event bridges classroom knowledge with practical problem-solving. It highlights creativity, experimentation, and scientific reasoning. Innovate showcases how young minds can use science to address modern challenges.

THE GROUP SINGING

HARMONY FOR TOMORROW

Harmony for Tomorrow celebrates unity and musical expression through group performance. Teams sing pieces aligned with themes like “Voices for Peace” or “Echoes of Innovation.” Participants blend harmony, rhythm, and emotion to create impactful musical stories. The event values meaningful lyrics, vocal coordination, and artistic interpretation. Harmony for Tomorrow shows how collective voices can inspire hope, connection, and creativity.
