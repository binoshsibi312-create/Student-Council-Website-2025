Bangalore Central Campus

Hosur Road, Near Diary Circle, Bangalore - 560029

Bangalore Central Campus offers a wide range of programmes, starting from Bachelors to PhD, in several domains. There are several other Centres and Associations in the campus which provide the students an opportunity to indulge in extra curricular and co-curricular activities.

Bangalore Bannerghatta Road Campus

Bannerghatta Main Road, Hulimavu, Bangalore - 560076

The BGR Campus was established in 2016. The programmes are structured in a way to help students acquire blended learning of different disciplines primarily concentrating on the roots of the main discipline under study.

Bangalore Kengeri Campus

Kanmanike, Kumbalgodu, Mysore Road, Bangalore - 560074

The Kengeri Campus was established in 2009 and provides programmes in Engineering, Architecture, Management and many more. The "eco-friendly" green campus is equipped with resources and infrastructure appropriate for a multicultural community, academic progress, research, and the latest advancements in the technical and digital world.

CLICK HERE TO KNOW MORE

Bangalore Yeshwanthpur Campus

Nagasandra, Near Tumkur Road, Bangalore - 560073

Upholding the Vision, Excellence and Service, and realizing the Mission through holistic education and inculcating Core Values, CHRIST Yeshwanthpur Campus with innovative and contemporary curricula and pedagogy and a refreshing environment conducive to teaching, learning, and research aims to create holistic individuals. It is 10 stories with over sixteen lakhs square feet, a space where architecture, technology and art merge, creating a future-focused learning environment next door to Dasrahalli/Nagasandra Metro Stations on Tumkur Road, 5 kms from Yeshwanthpur Railway station, and is set to function from August 2022.

Pune Lavasa Campus

30 Valor Court, Dasve Lavasa, Mulshi, Pune - 412112

The Pune Lavasa Campus was established in 2007 and focuses on providing Data Analytic oriented programmes to the students. Apart from these, several other Bachelor's and Master's programme are also provided.

Delhi NCR Campus

Mariam Nagar, Meerut Road, Delhi NCR, Ghaziabad - 201003

The NCR Campus was established in 2013 and it aims to provide the students a wide range of courses to choose from. The Campus offers courses from Sciences, Business & Management and Law.
