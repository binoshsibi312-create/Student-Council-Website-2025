DAKSH 2025

Education and Career Guidance Fair

DAKSH is an annual Education and Career Guidance Fair hosted by The Student Council, CHRIST (Deemed to be University). It focuses on the various opportunities made available to pre-university, undergraduate, and postgraduate students. DAKSH is here to guide students to acquire knowledge and make informed choices regarding their higher education and careers.

Through active participation in the stalls, several competitions, as well as personalized career counseling sessions, students will be able to learn about various vocations and fields of study they could pursue.

Why Attend DAKSH?

Are You Facing These Challenges?

  • Finding it difficult to answer questions about your career plans?
  • Feeling overwhelmed with the variety of choices offered by institutions?
  • Need better clarity regarding a career choice?
Then DAKSH is the perfect opportunity for you!

How We Help

  • Direct interaction with students and faculty to address your queries
  • Guidance on academics and extracurricular activities
  • Information about admission processes and requirements
  • Insights into placement opportunities and career paths
  • Understanding campus culture and student life
