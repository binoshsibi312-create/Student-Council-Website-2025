Competitions at Daksh 2025

Participate in exciting competitions across various domains and showcase your talents. Win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your skills.

The DEBATE

REASONED EVOLUTION

Reasoned Revolution is a unique debate competition designed to spark thought-provoking conversations and test students' critical thinking and oratory skills. Participants are judged on their arguments and delivery, making it a chance to build confidence, sharpen thinking, and leave an impact. Seasoned debater or first timer—this is your moment to be bold and be heard!

THE QUIZ

CHRONICLER

Enter the thrilling world of Chronicler: The Quiz, where quick wit, sharp knowledge, and keen perception collide. With four dynamic rounds, this quiz tests your grasp of current affairs, general knowledge, and on-the-spot thinking, tailored for school and PU college students. Whether you're a trivia enthusiast or seeking a challenge, Chronicler offers a fun yet competitive environment to learn, strategize, and win.

THE BUSINESS SIMULATION

PITCH PERFECT

Pitch Perfect is a thrilling business plan competition for students in Grades 9-12, where teams of 3-4 showcase creativity, strategy, and problem-solving. Round 1, Pitch Butter Masala, challenges teams to present their business plans while tackling surprise constraints like "zero marketing budget." In Round 2, Professor Tank, teams pitch to faculty acting as investors to earn "Daksh Coins." Step up, adapt, and prove you’re the next entrepreneurial star!

THE DANCE EVENT

NRITYA KSHETRA

Welcome to Nritya Kshetra, the ultimate dance arena where talent, creativity, and passion collide! In Round 1, teams dazzle with pre-prepared performances to iconic Indian film songs. Round 2 turns up the heat with an improv dance battle, as 4 dancers from each team go head-to-head in electrifying challenges. The Final Round pushes the top 5 teams to deliver spontaneous performances to assigned songs, testing their skill and adaptability. Get ready for breathtaking moves, fierce competition, and unmatched energy. Nritya Kshetra—where dancers become legends

THE SCIENCE FAIR

INNOVATE

INNOVATE is where curiosity meets creativity and science takes center stage! The event begins with Team Presentations, where groups of 3 students showcase groundbreaking projects in just 5 minutes, impressing an expert panel and the audience with their innovative ideas. Following this, an exclusive Career Guidance Workshop by an industry expert explores exciting science careers, scholarships, and pathways to turn passion into profession. At INNOVATE, it’s all about transforming ideas into real-world impact!

THE FILM FESTIVAL

DIRECTOR'S CUT

Unleash your creativity at Director’s Cut, a short film competition for Bangalore school students. Participants will receive a theme three weeks in advance to craft a 15-20 minute film showcasing their storytelling, filmmaking, and technical skills. Films from all genres are welcome and must be submitted a week prior to the event. The two-day competition features film screenings on Day 1, with winners announced on Day 2. Teams will also take on the AdVenture Challenge, creating ad films in just 2 hours. Show your passion and talent for film at Director’s Cut!
